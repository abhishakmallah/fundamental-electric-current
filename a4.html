Electric Current

Electric current is the flow of electric charge. It is measured in amperes (A). The formula for electric current is:

Current (I) = Charge (Q) / Time (t)

Calculate Current

Voltage

Voltage (or potential difference) is the force that pushes electric charges to flow in a circuit. It is measured in volts (V). The formula for voltage is:

Voltage (V) = Current (I) × Resistance (R)

Calculate Voltage

Power

Power is the rate at which work is done or energy is transferred. It is measured in watts (W). The formula for power is:

Power (P) = Voltage (V) × Current (I)

Calculate Power

Work & Energy

Work done or energy transferred by electric current is given by:

Work (W) = Power (P) × Time (t)
